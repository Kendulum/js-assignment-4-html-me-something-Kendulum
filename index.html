How to make toast!

The 5 steps to making toast are:

  • Step 1: Insert Bread Into Toaster
  • Step 2: Select Temperature
  • Step 3: Start The Toaster Magic
  • Step 4: Butter The Toast
  • Step 5: Enjoy The Toast!

Step 1: Insert Bread Into Toaster

Locate the toaster and bread. Take the bread out of the bag and place it into the toaster.

Step 2: Select Temperature

Toasters often have different temperature options. Select the one best fitted for your taste.

Step 3: Start The Toaster Magic

Press the button to initiate Toaster Magic down to the ground.

Step 4: Butter The Toast

Once the Toaster Magic is finished, pop the Toast out and spread it with butter or jam.

Step 5: Enjoy The Toast!

Enjoy the fruit of your labor like a normal person or like Mark Zuckerberg®! Probably not like Mark Zuckerberg® though.
